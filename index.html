Garlic Butter Pasta

This Garlic Butter Pasta is a simple yet flavorful dish perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Made with just a handful of pantry staples, it combines the richness of butter with the bold aroma of fresh garlic to create a comforting, satisfying meal in under 20 minutes. Whether served on its own or paired with your favorite protein, it’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or pasta of choice
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Add the drained pasta back into the pot with the garlic butter. Toss to coat the pasta evenly.
  4. If the pasta seems dry, add a little reserved pasta water to achieve your desired consistency.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
